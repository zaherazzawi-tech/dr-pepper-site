Our Heritage

Born Bold.
Still Going.

Created by pharmacist Charles Alderton in Waco, Texas, Dr Pepper is America's oldest major soft drink brand. Alderton mixed his secret formula of 23 flavors at Wade Morrison's Old Corner Drug Store, and the world has never been the same.

1885
Charles Alderton invents Dr Pepper in Waco, Texas at Wade Morrison's Old Corner Drug Store.
1904
Dr Pepper debuts to 20 million people at the World's Fair in St. Louis, Missouri.
1951
The iconic "10-2-4" slogan takes America by storm, encouraging drinkers to enjoy Dr Pepper three times a day.
2023
Dr Pepper surpasses Pepsi to become the #2 soft drink in America — a bold new chapter.
