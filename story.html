Born Bold. Still Going.

The Timeline

1885
The Birth of Bold
Pharmacist Charles Alderton invents Dr Pepper at Wade Morrison's Old Corner Drug Store in Waco, Texas. The unique 23-flavor formula is an instant hit with locals.
●
●
1891
First Company Founded
The Artesian Mfg. & Bottling Company is established — the first Dr Pepper bottling company in the world, laying the groundwork for national expansion.
1904
World's Fair Debut
Dr Pepper is introduced to over 20 million people at the World's Fair in St. Louis, Missouri — the same fair that introduced the ice cream cone and hot dog bun to America.
●
●
1951
The 10-2-4 Era
The iconic "Drink a bite to eat at 10, 2, and 4" campaign takes hold across America, encouraging people to drink Dr Pepper three times a day for an energy boost.
1972
I'm a Pepper
The legendary "I'm a Pepper" ad campaign launches, becoming one of the most recognized slogans in advertising history and cementing Dr Pepper's cultural icon status.
●
●
2008
Dr Pepper Snapple Group
Keurig Dr Pepper is formed, bringing together dozens of beloved beverage brands under one roof while keeping Dr Pepper's unique identity front and center.
2023
America's #2 Soda
Dr Pepper surpasses Pepsi to become the second most popular soft drink in the United States — a historic milestone 138 years in the making.
